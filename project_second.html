Cosmetic Sales Insights: Click Stream Data Analysis (Oct-Nov 2019)

This study utilizes a dataset obtained from an undisclosed online cosmetic retail company, comprising 'Click Stream Data' acquired through visitor click tracking on their website. The dataset encompasses data from October and November 2019. Employing HQL (Hive Query Language), the analysis aims to address eight specific inquiries, seeking to derive valuable insights and discern patterns that could enhance the company's sales performance. Additionally, the study endeavors to gain a comprehensive understanding of the financial implications, encompassing profit and loss assessments for the aforementioned months.
